# 👥 Retention Radar: HR Attrition Insights

> From reactive HR reporting to proactive retention strategy — powered by data.

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI capstone project, completed as part of the **Machinfy Professional Data Analytics Bootcamp**, analyzes HR employee data to uncover the root drivers of staff attrition and translate those findings into actionable retention strategies.

The core question: **Why are employees leaving — and what can we do before they do?**

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

High employee attrition is costly — in recruitment, training, and lost institutional knowledge. This project moves beyond simply reporting *who* left to understanding *why* they left, enabling HR teams to intervene proactively.

---

## 🔧 Approach & Methodology

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Data Cleaning | Cleaned and transformed raw HR data using Power Query |
| Feature Engineering | Categorized Distance Level, Age Group, and Experience Bands |
| DAX Measures | Built metrics for attrition rate, average salary, and department performance |
| Dashboard Design | Created interactive dashboards for real-time attrition monitoring |

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- ⚠️ **Early Career Risk**: Employees with **1–3 years** experience face the highest attrition rates
- 🔥 **Overtime Impact**: Overtime employees are **twice as likely** to leave
- ✈️ **Travel Factor**: Frequent travelers show the highest attrition rate across all groups
- 🏢 **Department Alert**: Research & Development shows a significant attrition concentration
- 👤 **Age Group**: Employees aged **26–35** exhibit the highest turnover rate
- 📍 **Location Surprise**: Employees living closest to the office show unexpectedly high attrition
